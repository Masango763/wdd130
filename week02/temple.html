Abidjan Côte d'Ivoire Temple

The Abidjan Côte d'Ivoire Temple is the first temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints built in Ivory Coast. The temple was announced in 2015 and dedicated in May 2025. It serves members of the Church in Ivory Coast and nearby West African countries.

Temples are sacred buildings where members of the Church perform important ordinances and make covenants with God. The Abidjan temple represents the growth of the Church in West Africa and provides a place of worship for members in the region.
