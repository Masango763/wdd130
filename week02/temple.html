Temples of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Abidjan Ivory Coast Temple

About the Temple

The Abidjan Ivory Coast Temple is the first temple built in Côte d'Ivoire. It serves members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the region and stands as a symbol of faith and commitment.

Dedicated 25 May 2025: Temple Information

Temple Information

  • Location: Abidjan, Côte d'Ivoire
  • Status: Operating
  • Region: Africa West Area
  • Purpose: Sacred worship and ordinances
