Abidjan Côte d'Ivoire Temple

About the Temple

The Abidjan Ivory Coast Temple is the seventh temple built in Africa and the first temple for Ivory Coast (Côte d'Ivoire).

Dedicated on 25 May 2025: Dedicatory Prayer
